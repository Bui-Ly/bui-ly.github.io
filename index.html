Common Topics

What support can help with

Accounts and sign-in

If sign-in, account recovery, or account-linked features are not working as expected, send the app name and a short step-by-step description of the problem.

Purchases and App Store issues

If a purchase is delayed or content does not appear, include the app name and transaction details. Refund requests are reviewed directly by Apple through reportaproblem.apple.com.

Bugs and unexpected behavior

For crashes, freezes, sync issues, or feature problems, include the exact device, OS version, app version, and what happened before the issue appeared.

Best Report

What to include in your email

  • The exact app name
  • Your device model, such as iPhone or iPad model
  • Your iOS or iPadOS version
  • The app version you are using
  • A short explanation of what happened and what you expected
  • Screenshots or video if available
