Troubleshooting

Common issues

Sign in with Apple is not working

Make sure the device has internet access, iCloud is enabled, and Sign in with Apple is available for your Apple ID. If the issue continues, close and reopen the app, then try again.

Coins or item packs did not appear

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. If a purchase looks delayed, wait a moment and reopen the app. If the charge went through but content was not delivered, contact support with the transaction details.

How do refunds work?

Refund requests for App Store purchases are reviewed directly by Apple. Use Apple's official refund page: reportaproblem.apple.com .
